Camera Stream for SignAI

How to Use:

  1. Allow SignAI to access your camera.
  2. Press the "Start Recording" button and wait for 3 seconds, then begin signing.
  3. When you're finished, wait another 3-4 seconds and press the "Stop Recording" button.

What is SignAI?

SignAI is an innovative project that uses a Seq2Seq LSTM AI model to translate sign language into text and vice versa.

The goal is to enhance communication between people with and without hearing impairments by breaking down the barriers between sign language and spoken language.
