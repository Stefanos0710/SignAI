About SignAI

SignAI is an innovative project that leverages advanced AI technology to bridge the communication gap between sign language users and others. Our mission is to make communication more accessible and inclusive for everyone.

Using machine learning algorithms and computer vision, SignAI provides real-time, accurate translation of sign language to text, making communication seamless and natural.
