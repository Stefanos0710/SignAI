SignAI

SignAI is an innovative AI-based application that bridges the communication gap between the hearing and the deaf communities. By using advanced machine learning models, SignAI can recognize sign language in real time – transforming gestures into spoken words and vice versa.

Get Started Learn More

About

Our Mission

Our mission is to make communication accessible for everyone, no matter their language or ability. SignAI bridges the gap between spoken and sign languages through advanced artificial intelligence. By combining cutting-edge computer vision, natural language processing, and deep learning, SignAI can understand and translate gestures, facial expressions, and context with remarkable accuracy.

Advanced Technology

Trained on tens of thousands of video samples from diverse, large-scale datasets, SignAI continues to improve through ongoing research and user feedback. Its adaptive learning model enables it to recognize subtle variations in regional sign languages and the personal signing styles of individuals.

Building Bridges

Our goal goes beyond translation — we aim to build a world where communication barriers no longer exist. Whether it's in education, public services, or daily life, SignAI empowers people to connect, understand, and share their voices freely.

Desktop

The desktop version of SignAI provides a powerful environment for testing, recording, and analyzing sign language. It allows users to record custom videos and instantly receive accurate translations.

With our desktop application, you get access to advanced features including real-time processing, detailed analytics, and the ability to save and review your translation history. The intuitive interface makes it easy for both beginners and professionals to communicate effectively.

Mobile

Our mobile version of SignAI brings the power of real-time translation directly to your pocket. Using your smartphone's camera and flash, the app delivers incredible results and enables seamless communication anywhere.

The mobile app is designed with accessibility in mind, offering a clean and simple interface that works in any lighting condition. Whether you're at home, at work, or on the go, SignAI Mobile ensures you can communicate effectively without barriers.
